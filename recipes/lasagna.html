Lasagna Recipe

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the ingredients:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Garlic and Onion
  • Tomato
  • Sugar, Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Tiramisu Cake Recipe

Description

Tiramisu cake is a fun twist on classic Italian tiramisu. It consists of cake layers soaked in coffee syrup layered with a mascarpone filling and creamy icing. This easy recipe calls for a package of white cake mix.

Ingredients

  1. For the cake
    • White cake mix
    • Water
    • Egg whites
    • Vegetable oil
    • Instant coffee powder
  2. For the coffee syrup
    • Coffee
    • Coffee-flavoured liqueur
  3. For the filling
    • Mascarpone cheese
    • Confectioners' sugar
    • Coffee-flavoured liqueur
  4. For the garnishes
    • Unsweetened cocoa powder
    • Semi-sweet chocolate

  5. Assemble the tiramisu cake.
  6. Garnish the cake and chill before serving.
